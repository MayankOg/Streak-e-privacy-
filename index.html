Privacy Policy for Streak-E

Effective Date: August 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Phone number and email address for account verification
  • Device information (type, OS, unique identifiers)
  • Usage data (in-app actions, streak activity)
  • Location data (with permission)
  • Camera & media access for snap streaks

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide services, improve app performance, recommend nearby merchants, enable streak features, and communicate important updates.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your data. Some third-party services may access minimal data for cloud hosting or analytics. We may disclose information if required by law.

4. Data Security

All information is encrypted in transit. While we follow best practices, no system can guarantee 100% security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary. Deleted accounts are removed within a reasonable time, except where law requires retention.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access and update personal info in the app
  • Delete your account at any time
  • Manage permissions (camera, location) in device settings
  • Request deletion of specific data

7. Account & Data Deletion Requests

You can request account deletion or specific data deletion using the forms below. We will process your request within 30 days.

Account Deletion Request

Warning: This will permanently delete your account and all associated data including streaks, photos, and progress. This action cannot be undone.

Specific Data Deletion Request

Use this form to request deletion of specific types of data while keeping your account active.
Processing Time: We will acknowledge your request within 3 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

8. Child Safety and Protection Policy

8.1 Age Restrictions and Verification

Streak-E is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete such information from our servers.

8.2 Child Abuse and Exploitation Prevention

Zero Tolerance Policy: Streak-E has a strict zero-tolerance policy against child abuse, exploitation, and any content that sexualizes, grooms, or harms minors.

Our Commitment to Child Safety:

  • Content Monitoring: All user-generated content, including photos and messages, is subject to automated and manual review for inappropriate content involving minors
  • AI Detection Systems: We employ advanced AI systems to detect and flag potential child exploitation material (CSAM) and grooming behaviors
  • Immediate Action: Suspected child abuse content results in immediate account suspension and law enforcement notification
  • Secure Reporting: Multiple channels for reporting suspected abuse with 24/7 monitoring
  • Staff Training: All team members receive specialized training in recognizing signs of child exploitation and abuse

8.3 Safety Measures for Younger Users (13-17)

For users aged 13-17, we implement additional protections:

  • Enhanced privacy settings by default
  • Restricted interaction capabilities with unknown users
  • Mandatory parental consent for certain features
  • Limited data collection and sharing
  • Regular safety reminders and educational content
  • Automatic content filtering for age-inappropriate material

8.4 Reporting and Response Procedures

🛡️ Report Child Safety Concerns

Emergency: If a child is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services (911, 112, etc.) before using this form.
Providing contact information helps us follow up if we need additional details.
Report Processing:
  • Immediate danger reports: Reviewed within 1 hour
  • Urgent reports: Reviewed within 4 hours
  • High priority: Reviewed within 24 hours
  • Standard priority: Reviewed within 72 hours

8.5 Law Enforcement Cooperation

Streak-E fully cooperates with law enforcement agencies regarding child safety:

  • Immediate reporting of CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Preservation of evidence for law enforcement investigations
  • Compliance with legal requests and court orders
  • Proactive sharing of information when required to protect children

8.6 Educational Resources

We provide resources to help keep children safe online:

  • In-app safety tips and guidelines
  • Links to organizations like Internet Safety 101, ConnectSafely, and NetSmartz
  • Regular safety communications to parents and guardians
  • Partnerships with child safety organizations
